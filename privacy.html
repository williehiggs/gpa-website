Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Global Profit Advisors (“GPA SMS”), a service of Kairos International Distributions LLC, is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, prospects, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (gpasms.com) or use our services.

What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal information you provide: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and any details you share via our contact forms, booking pages, onboarding forms, or direct communication with our team.

Usage data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other analytics data collected through cookies and tracking technologies.

Service-related data: Campaign performance data, response data, and account information necessary to deliver our services to active clients.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

Respond to inquiries and communicate with you about our services; schedule and confirm appointments; deliver and manage our SMS, cold calling, cold email, and data procurement services; send service-related notifications including onboarding updates, account notifications, and campaign status updates; improve our website and service offerings; comply with legal obligations.

SMS Opt-In & Consent

When you provide your phone number through our website forms (including our contact page and booking page), onboarding forms, or direct communication with our team, you may consent to receive SMS messages from Global Profit Advisors (GPA SMS), a service of Kairos International Distributions LLC.

These messages are service-related only and may include: appointment confirmations, onboarding updates, service status notifications, account-related communications, and follow-up messages related to your inquiry or active engagement with our team.

Message frequency varies based on your level of engagement with our services. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS communications will be sent.

For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact us at Tyler@globalprofitadvisors.com or 502-237-8159.

No Sharing of SMS Opt-In Consent

Your SMS opt-in consent information is not shared with, sold to, rented to, or distributed to any third party for their own marketing purposes or any other purpose. Consent data is collected and used solely by Global Profit Advisors (Kairos International Distributions LLC) to send the service-related messages you have authorized. We do not share, sell, or transfer your opt-in consent or phone number to any outside party under any circumstances.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, collect usage data, and support our marketing efforts. These may include first-party analytics cookies and third-party tracking pixels. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Client campaign data is retained for the duration of the service agreement and a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
